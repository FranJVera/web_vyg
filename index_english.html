VERA Y GALLEGO ENERGíA



Vera & Gallego Energía is a private company located in Zujaira (Pinos Puente, Granada) dedicated to the management and processing of biomass made from different kinds of local trees, mainly olive trees and black poplars.


It was founded in 2009 and has carried out several and diverse operations, such as supplying electricity to national companies or exportations, processing tens of millions of kilos per year.










PRUNING COLLECTION

A service of cleaning croplands is also provided by Vera & Gallego Energía, in order to collect
the pruning and transport it to the storage yard.



















FACILITIES AND INFRASTUCTURE


SUPPLY TO POWER PLANTS

The company owns a wide storage yard as well as all the necessary infrastructure for the collection, storage, processing and transportation of the biomass.




Biomass is classified according to types and is processed to obtain a considerable heat of combustion and a customised grading.

The supply of material is carried out in a flexible way, meeting the needs of the clients.



ELECTRICITY PRODUCTION



Vera & Gallego Energía develops its own electrical generation system which is still at an experimental stage.







SUSTAINABILITY

Vera & Gallego Energía has a strong compromise with the environment and strives to preserve it. For instance, collecting the pruning helps maintain forests and croplands as well as it is used as a renewable fuel.


Furthermore, Vera & Gallego Energía and the oil mill Vera, which colaborate jointly, have the necessary infrastructures for the complete use of the olive tree, with full respect for the environmental surroundings and the life cycle of the trees.
